Sobha Magnus is a premium residential development by Sobha Group located in Kothanur Village, Uttarahalli Hobli, South Bangalore. Spread across 5.85 acres, the project offers 294 luxury 3 & 4 BHK apartments with sizes ranging from 1856 to 2577 sq.ft — ideal for families seeking spacious, premium homes in a well-connected South Bangalore neighbourhood.

Registered with Karnataka RERA under number PRM/KA/RERA/1251/310/PR/131025/008160, Sobha Magnus is fully compliant and offers buyers complete transparency and legal protection. With apartments starting at ₹3.33 Cr* and possession scheduled for December 2028, this project is an excellent opportunity for families and investors looking for a quality home in South Bangalore's growing residential corridor near Kanakapura Road and NICE Road.
