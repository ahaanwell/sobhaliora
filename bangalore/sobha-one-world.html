Sobha One World is a green-themed master township by Sobha Limited spread across approximately 300 acres in Hoskote, East Bangalore, located just 700 metres from the Hoskote Toll Plaza on Old Madras Road, off the Raghuvanahalli - Hosakote Road, with access to the Kadugudi Metro Station. Within this township, the launched residential phase - One Residences - is spread across 48 acres 15.32 guntas and will offer 3,484 luxury 1, 2, 3 & 4 BHK apartments across 14 towers, developed in 6 phases. Each tower is configured as 3 basements + ground floor + 1st floor + 45/46 typical floors. Possession of Phase 1 is scheduled from 1 December 2032, with the remaining phases following RERA-registered timelines between July 2032 and September 2033.
