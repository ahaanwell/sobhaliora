Sobha Neopolis is an ultra-luxury, Greek/Santorini-themed high-rise residential township by Sobha Limited on Panathur Road, off Outer Ring Road, Bangalore. Spread across approximately 25.9 acres (104,801 sqm), the township comprises 19 residential wings - 18 wings of 2 basements + ground + 18 floors, and Wing 5 at 3 basements + ground + 18 floors, each standing approximately 57.6 metres tall. BBMP-sanctioned plans permit 1,886 units (developer collateral references ~1,875 units), offering 1 BHK, 3 BHK, 3 BHK + Study and 4 BHK configurations with saleable areas ranging from approximately 660 sq ft to 2,481 sq ft. The development utilises a sanctioned FAR of 2.99 (against a permissible 3.00) with a ground coverage of just 18.91% against a permissible 50%, leaving close to 78% of the parcel as open space.

Sobha Neopolis Master Plan & Project Layout

Sobha Neopolis is planned across ~25.9 acres (104,801 sqm; A-Khata 26 ac 06 g) as a 19-wing township. 18 of the wings are configured as 2 basements + ground + 18 floors, while Wing 5 rises to 3 basements + ground + 18 floors, with every tower standing approximately 57.6 metres tall.
