Sobha Ayana is a premium pre-launch residential project by Sobha Limited, located in Panathur, one of Bangalore's most sought-after residential micro-markets on the Outer Ring Road corridor. Spread across 6.28 acres with 683 well-designed 3 BHK apartments, the project is planned across 12–14 residential blocks each rising to 2B + G + 14 floors. Apartment sizes range from 1553 sq.ft to 1789 sq.ft, making Sobha Ayana an ideal choice for families seeking spacious, modern homes in East Bangalore.

Launched in November 2024 and RERA approved under registration number PR/030824/006958, Sobha Ayana offers early buyers an attractive entry price starting at Rs. 2.46 Cr* with possession expected by September 2027. As with all Sobha Limited projects, the development promises high-quality construction, thoughtful design and a lifestyle-centric community.
