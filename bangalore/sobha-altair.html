Sobha Altair is a premium residential development by Sobha Limited located on Sarjapur Road, Bangalore. Spread across 3.37 acres, the project offers 290 luxury 3, 3.5 & 4 BHK apartments across 3 basements + ground + 18 floors, supported by 310 covered and 21 open parking spaces. The project is approved by the Bangalore Development Authority (BDA) and is RERA registered under No. PRM/KA/RERA/1251/446/PR/070126/008388. Sarjapur Road is one of Bangalore's most sought after residential corridors, offering strong connectivity to the Outer Ring Road IT belt, Whitefield, Electronic City and HSR Layout.
